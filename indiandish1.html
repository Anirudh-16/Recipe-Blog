DOSA WITH COCONUT CHUTNEY
INGREDIENTS




* Dosa Batter
1 cup urad dal (no skin)
3 cups rice flour
Pinch sugar
Salt


* Coconut Chutney
1/2 cup frozen shredded coconut
1/4 cup roasted chana dal
1/4 cup dry coconut powder
5 Thai chiles
1 small whole tamarind
1/2 bunch fresh cilantro
Salt
Oil, for cooking
Ghee, for topping




PROCEDURE


1. For the dosa batter: Soak urad dal in water for 8 to 12 hours. Wash the soaked lentils and blend in a high-powered blender with 3/4 cup water to a smooth paste. Add rice flour, sugar, salt and 1 1/2 cups water to blend to a smooth paste consistency. Let batter ferment at room temperature 8 to 10 hours.

2. For the coconut chutney: Add coconut, chana dal, coconut powder, chiles, tamarind seeds, cilantro and salt to a high-powdered blender with water and blend to a smooth paste consistency. Finish by adding a tablespoon of tempering to the coconut chutney.

3. Heat a cast-iron or nonstick pan over medium heat and season with oil. Once it is hot, take one ladle of dosa batter and spread it from the middle to outer edge of the pan, making sure it is thin. Cook until cooked completely, about 1 minute. Put ghee on top of dosa. Serve with potato masala and coconut chutney. Repeat with remaining batter.
